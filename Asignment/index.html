”Frequently Asked Questions”

Everything you need to know before booking your ticket.

When and where is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 runs March 14–16 at the Moscone Center in San Francisco. Doors open at 8:00 AM each day, with keynotes starting at 9:00 AM.

Can I switch ticket tiers after registering?

Yes. You can upgrade from Standard to Pro anytime before the event. Contact our support team and pay only the price difference.

Are workshops included in every ticket?

All attendees can join open workshops. Pro and Team tickets include access to exclusive, limited-seat deep-dive sessions with speakers.

Is there a refund policy?

Full refunds are available until February 1, 2026. After that date, tickets can be transferred to another person at no extra cost.
